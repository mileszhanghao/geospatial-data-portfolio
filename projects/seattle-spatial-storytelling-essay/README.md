# Seattle Spatial Storytelling Essay

This essay synthesizes Seattle-focused data visualization work across physical geography, human experience, and algorithmic analysis.

## Focus

- Narrative cartography and urban spatial interpretation.
- Seattle's layered geography, infrastructure, and neighborhood patterns.
- The shift from curated public datasets to self-generated and less structured spatial data.
- How visualization choices affect public understanding of place.
